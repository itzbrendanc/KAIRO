# KAIRO Growth Plan

## Goal

Build KAIRO into an AI-powered trading research subscription business that can realistically grow toward $10,000 in monthly recurring revenue.

## Product focus

- Real-time stock research and market analysis
- AI buy, hold, and sell signals with transparent explanations
- Daily watchlist briefings and signal-change alerts
- Paper trading, journaling, and lessons for beginners
- Portfolio analytics and premium retention workflows

## Pricing model

- Free: public exploration, previews, simulation, limited saved workflows
- Pro: $39/month
- Pro+: $99/month

### $10k MRR target math

- 180 Pro users x $39 = $7,020
- 30 Pro+ users x $99 = $2,970
- Total = $9,990

## Growth engine

- Daily short-form content on TikTok, Instagram, and X
- Public product exploration before signup
- Free-to-paid conversion through saved watchlists, alerts, and portfolio tools
- Daily email brief and weekly review loops
- Community and learning features for retention

## Trust posture

- Educational and informational only
- No guaranteed-return language
- Transparent signal reasoning
- Honest labeling when live data is unavailable
- Visible risk disclaimers across core pages

## 30-day roadmap

### Week 1

- Tighten pricing, trust, and onboarding
- Improve signal explanations and stock detail pages

### Week 2

- Run beta testing and fix UX friction
- Add core activation and conversion analytics

### Week 3

- Launch publicly
- Start daily content and free trial conversion loop

### Week 4

- Scale top-performing growth channels
- Add referrals and stronger retention messaging
